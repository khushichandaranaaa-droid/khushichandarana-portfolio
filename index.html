About

Strategy first. Aesthetics always.

I help small and mid-sized brands build the kind of social presence usually reserved for companies with ten-person marketing teams — sharp strategy, consistent visual language, and content that actually converts.

Over three years I've worked across healthcare, sports, events, jewellery, and packaging — building content calendars, pitch decks, and campaign strategy for brands like House of Tooth, IFSC Bhopal, WSC Events, Manoj Ornaments, Harmony Silver Linings, Trigon Digipack, and Hair Cherry.

I completed my BCA in 2026 and hold certifications across digital marketing, cybersecurity, and AI tools — because good strategy today means understanding both the platforms and what's coming next.
